Case Studies

Concept project 1: App Blocker

In the one-week concept project, I was challenged with creating a mobile app for my classmate to aid them in their day to day lives. This project was about rapid prototyping, creating an interactive prototype of designs based on user research.

Concept project 2: Take Note Stationery

In weeks two and three we were tasked with creating a mid-fidelity prototype of an e-commerce website for a Stationery company. We used the lean UX model, Double Diamond, to achieve this. The project was about executing a full 2-week design process while applying fundamental UX skills.

Concept project 3: Unplug App

In fourth week, we went back to our projects from week one to work on the graphic design, and so developed our UI skills.

Concept project 4: Netflix Originals Micriosite

During a two-week concept project, my team and I were challenged with creating a microsite for Netflix originals. We created a high fidelity responsive prototype.
